Frequently Asked Questions

what is DigitalSnan?

Digital Snan is a service where our team physically performs Ganga snan (holy bath) in Prayagraj on your behalf using your uploaded photo. We print your photo, carry it to the sacred ghats, perform the snan ritual in the actual Ganga river, and send you a personalized video within a week.

Is my data safe with DigitalSnan?

Yes, we use 256-bit SSL encryption to ensure all your personal data and browsing history remain private and secure.

How do i receive my snan video?

You will receive the personalized video and photos via Instagram and email. The video will clearly show your photo and mention your name during the ritual. Standard delivery is within a week of booking confirmation.

Is my photo safe and private?

Absolutely. Your photos are used only for your ritual and to create your personalized video. We respect your privacy completely, never share photos publicly, and responsibly dispose of printed photos after the ritual is completed.
